Career Chronicles

Alita.A

Software Developer

I came to Bengaluru from Pune soon after graduating college. With a background in hard sciences and finance, I bring a diverse perspective that allows me to focus on delivering customer-obsessed solutions. Shortly after joining Gigleaz, I had the opportunity to contribute to several high-impact computer vision projects with my team. Now, I’m working on streamlining the process to train machine learning models.

My favorite part about Gigleaz is the level of autonomy given to employees. Being at Gigleaz is really all about what you make of it. You are the one in control of your career here.

Growing up in Maharashtra, I was always immersed in the arts and performed in choirs, theatre groups, and concert symphonies. This experience in music has instilled in me a drive to constantly self-improve and strive for excellence in both my personal and professional life. At home, I love cooking spicy foods, cruising on my longboard, and I’m an ardent Virtual Reality gamer.

 

— Alita, Software Developer
