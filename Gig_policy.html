Gigleaz Policy

Gigleaz Board Diversity Policy:

1. Purpose: This Board Diversity Policy outlines Gigleaz's commitment to diversity within its Board of Directors.
2. Scope: This Policy pertains specifically to the Board and does not extend to general employees.
3. Policy Statement: Gigleaz values and promotes diversity on its Board, recognizing the benefits of varied perspectives,
experiences, and skills. A diverse Board enhances decision-making, governance, and overall performance,
contributing to the company's strategic objectives and reputation.


The Nomination and Remuneration Committee is tasked with evaluating Board composition and performance, ensuring diversity, skills, and expertise are appropriately represented. Discrimination based on personal attributes unrelated to performance is strictly prohibited.


The Board composition complies with legal and regulatory requirements, maintaining a balance of executive, non-executive, and independent directors. Shareholders are provided with relevant information to assess Board diversity and composition, in accordance with laws and regulations.

1.Limitations, Review, and Amendment: In case of conflict with applicable laws, legal provisions supersede this Policy.
Any changes in relevant laws automatically apply to this Policy.
The Committee periodically reviews and recommends updates to ensure alignment with evolving requirements and best practices.


Code of Conduct for Ethics at Gigleaz:

1. Integrity: Demonstrate honesty, sincerity, and ethical behavior in all interactions and decision-making processes.
2. Respect: Treat all individuals with dignity, respect, and fairness, regardless of differences in background, status, or beliefs.
3. Confidentiality: Safeguard confidential information and respect privacy rights, ensuring sensitive data is handled securely and responsibly.
4. Accountability: Take ownership of actions and decisions, acknowledging mistakes, and striving to rectify them promptly.
5. Transparency: Foster open communication and transparency in all business dealings, providing accurate and truthful information to stakeholders.
6. Compliance: Adhere to all laws, regulations, and company policies governing ethical conduct, avoiding conflicts of interest and unethical practices.
7. Social Responsibility: Act responsibly towards society and the environment, supporting sustainable practices and initiatives that benefit communities and the planet.
8. Professionalism: Uphold professional standards of conduct in all professional interactions, maintaining integrity, competence, and confidentiality.
9. Ethical Leadership: Lead by example, promoting ethical behavior and values throughout the organization, and inspiring others to do the same.
10. Continuous Improvement: Continuously strive to enhance ethical awareness, knowledge, and practices, seeking opportunities for learning and development.
By adhering to this Code of Conduct for Ethics, Gigleaz employees contribute to a culture of integrity, trust, and ethical excellence, fostering a positive and sustainable work environment.


Whistleblower Policy at Gigleaz:

1. Purpose:
The Whistleblower Policy is designed to encourage employees, stakeholders, and third parties to report any suspected
misconduct, unethical behavior, or violations of company policies and laws without fear of retaliation.
2. Reporting Mechanisms:
Employees, stakeholders, and third parties may report concerns or complaints through designated channels, including but not limited to, email, hotline, or direct communication with designated personnel such as the Compliance Officer or Human Resources Department.
3. Confidentiality and Anonymity:
Reports made under this policy will be treated with the utmost confidentiality. Whistleblowers have the option to remain anonymous if they wish, and their identity will be protected to the fullest extent possible, unless disclosure is required by law or necessary for investigation purposes.
4. Non-Retaliation:
Gigleaz prohibits retaliation against individuals who report concerns or complaints in good faith. Any form of retaliation against whistleblowers will result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contractual relationship.
5. Investigation and Resolution:
Reports made under this policy will be promptly investigated in a fair, impartial, and thorough manner. Gigleaz will take appropriate action to address substantiated concerns, including implementing corrective measures and disciplinary action against individuals found to have engaged in misconduct.
6. Compliance:
All employees, stakeholders, and third parties are required to cooperate fully with investigations conducted under this policy and refrain from making false accusations. Failure to comply with this policy may result in disciplinary action.
7. Communication and Awareness:
Gigleaz will communicate the Whistleblower Policy to all employees, stakeholders, and third parties, ensuring awareness of reporting procedures and protections available under this policy.
8. Review and Updates:
This policy will be reviewed periodically to ensure its effectiveness and compliance with applicable laws and regulations. Any updates or revisions will be communicated to all relevant parties.
